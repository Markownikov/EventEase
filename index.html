Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is EventEase?

    EventEase is a web based application that can be used to host any conference events involving keynote sessions,paper submission etc, designed to deliver real engagement and best-in-class interactions.

  • How to Register for an Event at EventEase?

    Click on "Register" and enter the required details to register.After registration you can create or join a meeting.

  • How to host an event at EventEase?

    Login with your registered credentials. Click on "Host Meeting" and fill the necessary information regarding meeting nad event schedules.

  • What browsers are supported by EventEase?

    Supported browsers:

      Chrome version >= 79 (Recommended)
      MS edge all version
      Firefox (version >= 76)
      Brave Browser
      Safari (with limited access & known compatibility issue)

  • What's the quality of video streams in EventEase?

    EventEase supports High Definition video quality for live sessions and virtual table meetings.

      The High Definition(HD) is streamed in 720p resolution.
      The Low Definition(LD) is streamed in 360p resolution.
